# Code of Conduct

## Standard

This repository should remain professional, respectful, and useful.

Expected behavior:

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Focus critique on code, design, tradeoffs, and evidence.
- Respect boundaries and keep the project space professional.
- Keep public discussion suitable for a professional portfolio repository.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, exploit instructions, or unsafe operational details.
- Spam or off-topic promotion.

## Enforcement

The repository owner may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or restrict participation to keep the project safe and professional.
